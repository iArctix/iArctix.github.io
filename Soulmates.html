Soulmates


Synopsis

Soulmates is a 2d platformer created for the Lost relic game jam in one week with a friend.The theme for the game was connection so the players aim was to get the 2 characters to reach eachother in each level to complete it. The game has 2 stories with 2 differnt couples one developed by each of us which added new abilities and types of levels for the player to complete.My story was the Connor and Richael story and had a dash ability and a shrinking ability with levels catered to them

Key Contributions

The game was split into two story lines with my friend working on the lewis/dani and me the Connor/Richael as seen in the gallery some key parts of mine were as follows

  • 2 differnt charactyers created with differnt abilities, one with a dash ability and one whcih can shrink to fit in gaps.
  • Designing a group of levels which incorperate the need for each characters ability and add progressive challenge
  • Animating the character and adding particles to them for polish in game
  • Designing main menu and story select and all other gameplay ui elements in game
