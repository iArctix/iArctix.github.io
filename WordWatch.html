Word Watch


Synopsis

Word Watch is a mobile/ browser game currently in development by myself where i have challenged myself to cover all sides of development from art, sound design and coding. The game is a word finding game where the goal is to find words in the grid to build up a high score. The game draws inspiration from scrabble and word searches to create a nice brain training game. Users can also also complete quests and achievments and are able to see their best game and longest words on their profile.

Key Features

Some of the key features are as follows. See gifs on these in the gallery below

  • Word finding and scoring- Words are checked against a database of over 130000 words to chewkc if they are valid and points are worked out from dictionary of letetrs each with a point value
  • Data Persistance- Profiles, quests and acheivements all persist over game sessions allwoing players to build up and get progress over sessions
  • Achievments- Some difficulty achievments for players to accomplish and go for. Some will happen over time and some take lots of skill all of which are viewable in the achievemnts panel
  • Quests- Quests allow users to gain xp and quests are randomly generated from a list of quest types an dthe goal and rewrad are randomly genrated based on the goal/difficulty
  • Player Feedback- Player feedback implemented accross ther board with reds and green used of rvalid and invalid, tiles shaking if the word is invalid, and ui to show what letters are selected and if it forms a valid word
