Watch Your Step/h1>

Synopsis

Watch your step is a rage platformer game drawing inspiration from games such as Jump King and Getting over it. The player is tasked with getting to the top of the tower and getting the treasure in the last amount of jumps as possible. The game was created in one week for brackeys game jam 2022.1 by me and my friend.

Key Contributions


  • Unique movement system launching the player with power and angle values for the player to learn and get to grips with
  • Level design and creating mechanics that get slowly fed to the player as they get further into the game such as false blocks; slime blocks that can bounce the player and moving platforms
  • Player stats being tracked from completion %, time taken and jumps so players can try beat their scores on the next playthrough
