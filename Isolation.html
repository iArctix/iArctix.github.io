Isolation


Synopsis

Isolation is a space adventure game with one goal. Reach home... Explore a Vast, Open Galaxy with Planets, Resources and Inhabitants that may of may not want to kill you. Gather Fuel and Gold Nuggets to buy upgrades from these Strange Species to help you achieve your goal. Game was created in one week for Brackeys game jam 2022.2 in one week by me and my artist friend with everything being amde in house by us

Key Contributions

Some of my key Contributionsto the project are as follows

  • Diverse enemy AI system with different enemy and attack types. From passive aggressive and neutral species all with boss ships, bomber ships and regular fighters to make diverse combat
  • Unique dialogue and trading system with over 10,000 unique possibilities of npc to interact with offering different services
  • Procedurally generated planet spawning system with every playthrough being different and each planet having a chance to be vendor planets, aggresive planetor or neutral. All with different possibilities for the player
  • Smooth polished spaceship movement adn combat controlled by the mouse and using some intertia based physics to add the space feel to the spaceships while keeping quick fluent movement
